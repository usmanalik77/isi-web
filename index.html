About ISI

Creating a better student experience, since 1987

ISI is a student orginization for MSc students within the Departement of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Stavanger. Our members are enrolled in studies such as Computer Science, Robotics and Data Science. We currently represent almost 200 students. Since our founding in 1987, we have worked with promoting social and professional gatherings for our members, both on and off campus. At the university, we have our own room where our members can study or relax with a cup of coffee. We also promote connections between students and the business sector, by organizing company presentations and workshops for our members. ISI also informs students about external events and vacancies on behalf of relevant companies.
